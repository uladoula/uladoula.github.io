Hello, I'm Your Doula

I believe that every birthing person deserves compassionate, continuous support throughout their pregnancy and birth journey. My passion for birth work stems from a deep respect for the transformative power of childbirth and the strength of birthing families.

With specialized training and years of experience, I provide evidence-based support while honoring your unique preferences, values, and birth goals. Whether you're planning a home birth, hospital birth, or birth center delivery, I'm here to support your choices and help you feel empowered throughout the process.

My Philosophy

Person-Centered Care

Your birth experience is yours. I provide unbiased information and support to help you make decisions that align with your values and preferences.

Evidence-Based Support

I stay current with the latest research in maternal health and birth practices to provide you with accurate, reliable information.

Holistic Approach

I recognize that birth involves physical, emotional, and spiritual dimensions. My support addresses all aspects of your wellbeing.

Continuous Presence

During labor, I remain by your side, providing unwavering support and helping you navigate each stage of birth.
