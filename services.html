Services

Comprehensive Support for Your Birth Journey

I offer a range of personalized services designed to support you through pregnancy, birth, and the postpartum period. Each package can be customized to meet your unique needs and preferences.

Birth Doula Package

Complete Labor & Birth Support

What's Included:

  • Initial consultation meeting
  • 2-3 prenatal visits (2-3 hours each)
  • Continuous labor support from active labor through 2 hours postpartum
  • Birth plan development assistance
  • Access to lending library (books, birth ball, TENS unit, etc.)
  • Phone & email support throughout pregnancy
  • One postpartum visit within 2 weeks of birth
  • Birth story processing and debriefing
On-call period: From 38 weeks gestation until birth
